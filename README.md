# 🎮 Online Games

A collection of browser-based games that you can play instantly - no downloads required!

## 🚀 Current Games

### Space Defender
A classic space shooter where you defend Earth from alien invasions.

**How to Play:**
- Use **Arrow Keys** (← →) or **A/D** to move your spaceship
- Press **SPACE** to shoot
- Collect power-ups (health, rapid fire, shield)
- Survive as long as possible!

**Play Now:** [Click here to play Space Defender](https://aiuserchao.github.io/onlinegame/)
